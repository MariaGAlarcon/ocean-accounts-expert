# South-South Ocean Accounting Exchange 2026

**Dates:** 30 March to 1 April 2026
**Venue:** IPB International Convention Center, Bogor, Indonesia
**Hosts:** MMAF (Indonesia), IPB University, GOAP Secretariat, Rekam Nusantara Foundation

## Event summary

Three-day exchange convening early-career professionals, academics, and government stakeholders from Global South countries for hands-on ocean accounting, peer learning, and policy integration. Participants bring real datasets and policy questions, compile pilot ocean accounts, and co-develop policy briefs aligned with post-UNOC3 commitments, SDGs, NDCs, and NBSAPs.
