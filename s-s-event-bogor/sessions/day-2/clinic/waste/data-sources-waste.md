# Data Sources for Ocean Waste and Emissions Accounts

Where to find the data you need for each sub-account in the ocean waste and emissions framework (SEEA-CF physical flow accounts).

---

## Water Emissions to Sea

### National and local data

| Data source | What it provides | URL / contact |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------|
| National environment ministry | Wastewater discharge permits, effluent monitoring data, pollutant load estimates | Country-specific |
| Water utilities / sewerage authorities | Treatment plant volumes, influent and effluent quality, treatment level | Country-specific |
| EPA or environmental protection authority | Coastal water quality monitoring, compliance data | Country-specific |
| Agriculture ministry | Fertilizer use statistics, livestock numbers (for nutrient runoff estimates) | Country-specific |
| National water quality monitoring programs | Time series of pollutant concentrations at coastal stations | Country-specific |

### International and global data

| Data source | What it provides | URL |
|-------------|-----------------|-----|
| GEMS/Water (UNEP) | Global water quality database with station-level data | gemstat.org |
| WHO/UNICEF Joint Monitoring Programme (JMP) | Sanitation coverage and wastewater treatment rates by country | washdata.org |
| FAO AQUASTAT | Water withdrawal, wastewater generation, and treatment data by country | fao.org/aquastat |
| UNEP GEO Data Portal | Environmental indicators including water quality | geodata.gov |
| Global Nutrient Management Toolbox | Nutrient budgets and flows by country | nutrientchallenge.org |

## Solid Waste Entering the Sea

### National and local data

| Data source | What it provides | URL / contact |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Municipal waste management agencies | Waste generation, collection rates, composition, disposal methods | Country-specific |
| National statistics office | Household waste surveys, municipal waste statistics | Country-specific |
| Port authorities | Ship waste reception facility records (MARPOL Annex V compliance) | Country-specific |
| Fisheries agencies | Fishing gear loss reports, vessel waste management records | Country-specific |
| Coastal management agencies | Beach litter survey data, coastal cleanup records | Country-specific |

### International and global data

| Data source | What it provides | URL |
|-------------|-----------------|-----|
| Jambeck et al. (2015) | Country-level estimates of mismanaged plastic waste and marine debris inputs | science.sciencemag.org (doi: 10.1126/science.1260352) |
| Ocean Conservancy International Coastal Cleanup | Beach cleanup data by country, item type, and quantity | oceanconservancy.org |
| OSPAR Commission | Beach litter monitoring data and methodology (NE Atlantic region) | ospar.org |
| UNEP Global Partnership on Marine Litter | Reports, data compilations, and methodology guidance | unep.org/explore-topics/oceans-seas |
| World Bank What a Waste database | Municipal solid waste generation and management data by country | datatopics.worldbank.org/what-a-waste |
| Lebreton et al. (2017) | Modelled river plastic inputs to the ocean by river system | nature.com (doi: 10.1038/ncomms15611) |
| GESAMP Reports | Joint Group of Experts on Scientific Aspects of Marine Environmental Protection | gesamp.org |

## Air Emissions from Ocean Industries

### National and local data

| Data source | What it provides | URL / contact |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------|
| National GHG inventory (UNFCCC submissions) | Emissions by sector, including domestic navigation and fishing | Country-specific |
| Maritime authority | Vessel registry, fuel consumption records, ship type and size data | Country-specific |
| Port authorities | Port emission inventories, vessel movements, shore power data | Country-specific |
| Fisheries agencies | Fleet composition, vessel fuel consumption estimates | Country-specific |
| Petroleum/energy authority | Offshore platform emissions, flaring and venting data | Country-specific |

### International and global data

| Data source | What it provides | URL |
|-------------|-----------------|-----|
| IMO Fourth GHG Study (2020) | Shipping emission factors, global and regional shipping emissions | imo.org |
| UNFCCC National Inventory Submissions | Country GHG inventories including transport and energy sectors | unfccc.int |
| IEA Energy Statistics | Fuel consumption data by sector and country | iea.org |
| Global Fishing Watch | Vessel tracking data, fishing effort, and apparent fuel use | globalfishingwatch.org |
| ICCT (International Council on Clean Transportation) | Shipping emission analysis and policy research | theicct.org |
