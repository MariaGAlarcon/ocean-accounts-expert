# Data Sources for Ocean Economy Satellite Accounts

Where to find the data you need for each step of the OESA compilation process.

---

## Step 1: Define scope

### Geographic boundaries

| Data needed | Where to get it | URL |
|-------------|----------------|-----|
| EEZ boundaries (200 nm) | VLIZ Maritime Boundaries Geodatabase | marineregions.org |
| Territorial sea (12 nm) and contiguous zone (24 nm) | VLIZ Maritime Boundaries Geodatabase | marineregions.org |
| Coastal zone definitions | National coastal zone management legislation | Country-specific |
| Coastal municipality boundaries | National mapping agency, census geography | Country-specific |
| Bathymetry (ocean depth) | GEBCO global bathymetric chart | gebco.net |

### Economic scope references

## Step 4: Estimate ocean partials

### Fisheries and aquaculture (living resources)

| Data needed | Where to get it | URL |
|-------------|----------------|-----|
| Total catch (marine vs freshwater) | National fisheries agency, FAO FishStatJ | fao.org/fishery/statistics/fishstatj |
| Catch by country (if no local data) | Sea Around Us | seaaroundus.org |
| Aquaculture production | FAO FishStatJ, national aquaculture registry | fao.org/fishery |
| Fishing fleet registry | National maritime authority | Country-specific |
| Fish landing site data | Fisheries agency field offices | Country-specific |

### Country OESA benchmarks
