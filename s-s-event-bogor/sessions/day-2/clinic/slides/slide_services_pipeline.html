Ecosystem Services Pipeline

From ecosystem accounts and economic data to physical and monetary supply tables
Inputs

Extent Accounts

Area per ecosystem type (ha)

Condition Accounts

Ecosystem health (CI 0-1)

Economic Data

Prices, costs, revenues

Literature Rates

NCP rates, value transfer

Service Types

Provisioning

  • Fish and shellfish harvest
  • Mangrove wood and fibre
  • Seaweed / aquaculture

Regulating

  • Carbon sequestration
  • Coastal protection
  • Nursery habitat support
  • Water purification

Cultural

  • Tourism and recreation
  • Gleaning (subsistence)
  • Cultural and spiritual
Valuation Methods

Resource Rent

Revenue minus costs

Social Cost of Carbon

$51/tCO2 (US EPA)

Replacement Cost

Cost of built alternative

Direct Expenditure

Actual spending data

Value Transfer

Apply rates from similar sites

Outputs

Physical Supply Table

Service x ecosystem type x quantity (kg, Mg CO2, visits, km)

Monetary Supply Table

Service x ecosystem type x value ($/yr)
