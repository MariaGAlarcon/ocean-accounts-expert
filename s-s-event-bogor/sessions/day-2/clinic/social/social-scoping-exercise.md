# Social Account Scoping Exercise

This exercise helps you identify what social data already exists in your country that could feed into an ocean social account. It follows Stages 1-3 of the Social Account Development process from the GOAP Technical Guidance Circular.

This is **not a calculation exercise**. Social accounts are built through a process of co-design, data auditing, and consultation. This worksheet structures that process so you can take concrete first steps.
