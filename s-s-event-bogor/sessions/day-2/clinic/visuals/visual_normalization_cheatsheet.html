Condition Index: How to Normalize
Turn any indicator into a 0 – 1 score
Higher is better ▲
CI = Value / Reference
Use when more of the indicator means better condition. Examples: coral cover, fish biomass, species richness.
Higher is worse ▼
CI = 1 − (Value / Reference)
Use when more of the indicator means worse condition. Examples: macroalgae cover, turbidity, nutrient loading.
