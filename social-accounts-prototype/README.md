# Social Accounts Prototype

Working prototype for ocean social accounts, testing whether the conceptual framework from GOAP Technical Guidance Circular TG-3.5 translates into practical accounting tables with proper accounting identities.

## Status

Experimental. This is a brainstorming and design space, not a finished product.

## What this prototype tests

1. Whether directional logic chains from ecosystems through to social wellbeing hold together as accounting relationships
2. Whether household disaggregation can serve as the bridge unit connecting SNA economic accounts to social dimensions
3. Whether social stocks and flows can maintain accounting identities (opening + change = closing; supply = use) parallel to SEEA-EA ecosystem accounts
4. Which table formats from extent, condition, and service accounts transfer to social accounts and which need adaptation
