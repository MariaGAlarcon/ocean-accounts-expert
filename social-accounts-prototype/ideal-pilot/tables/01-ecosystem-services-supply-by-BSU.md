# Ecosystem Services Supply Table by BSU

**Accounting area:** Tuvana (national)
**Accounting period:** 2021-22
**Unit:** Physical units (service-specific)

This table records how much of each ecosystem service is supplied by each BSU. It is the spatial version of the standard SEEA-EA ecosystem services supply table.

| BSU-7 Lau South | 423 | 148 | 1,250 | 19,600 | 3,700 | Subsistence + cultural |

**The spatial pattern reveals the core insight:** Urban and peri-urban BSUs are dominated by market services (tourism, commercial fishing). Outer island BSUs are dominated by non-market services (subsistence fishing, gleaning, cultural). The standard economic accounts capture BSU-1 through BSU-3 well. Social accounts are needed to capture BSU-5 through BSU-7.
